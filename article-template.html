Warehouse Order sorting in Queues

Last updated: February 2026 • 8 min read

In this post, I delve deep behind the scenes and reveal a few secrets about how warehouse orders are technically being sorted in a queue.

Technical Sorting Criteria

By default, the EWM standard sorts the WOs within the queues based on two primary values saved in the table /SCWM/WO_RSRC_TY.

Figure 1: Evaluating the Latest Start Date (LSD) and Priority in the monitor.

Custom Logic via BAdI

If the standard sorting does not fit your business requirements, you can intervene using a specific enhancement point:

/SCWM/EX_RSRC_PROC_SEL

This allows you to resort the manual assigned warehouse orders or even filter them out based on custom resource constraints.
